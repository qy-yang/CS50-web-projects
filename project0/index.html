This website is inspired by the movie "The secret life of Walter Mitty".


We have selected three places: Greenland, Iceland and Himalayan that the protagonist has traveled in the movie and provide some information for those who are interested to travel there.

For many people, if I can't be a hero, if I can't get rid of the dull, at least I can let myself experience different life and different world, even if it is only temporary. This strange feeling grows stronger with growth.

The essence of the film is dreaming. Dreams allow you to have vision, touch, hearing, smell and immersiveness. Crossing in dreams and real life is still a profound artistic means.
